My Story

How I Got Involved in Tech

My name is Bargavi Kanneganti and coding found me through a series of coding meetups in Seoul, South Korea. What was once a passing interest became an urge to learn more. I later learned of the unsettling rise of child abuse online through a speech by actress Blake Lively, who talked of how the Child Rescue Coalition's fight to protect children around the globe.

My motivation emboldened me to protect users, especially young users, from the dangers of the dark side of the online world. Inspired by software engineers working at Thorn and the Child Rescue Coalition, I did not waste anytime leaping into the world of tech to make a difference.
